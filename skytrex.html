Sungai Congkak

Location: Hulu Langat, Kuala Selangor.
This latest and newest park is located in the cool and hilly area of Hulu Langat. Take a leisurely drive through the quaint town of
Hulu Langat with charming food stalls and fruits stands on both side of the road and be transported back in time to a picturesque village
setting with rivers and waterfalls amidst a lush tropical orchards and forest.

The park is operated by paying great respect to the environment and will enforce restrictions especially on usage of single use plastic
products and styrofoam. Please read all the information on your confirmation email and signages available at the park.

Online purchase is required to ensure you secure a space for your activity. All payments are to be made online via internet
banking or credit cards. If you “Walk-in” on the day of activity, you will run the risk of not getting a slot to participate
in the activity. So purchase your tickets first before going to the park
