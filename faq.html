Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How can we help you ? Got questions to ask ? Feel free to take a look into our mostly frequently asked questions below.



Question 1 : Are reservations necessary ?

It is highly recommended as it saves you time and the sign-up process can be much shorter.





Question 2 : Extreme activity tips, what to bring ?

Appropriate clothing for the weather, dry change of clothes, sneakers or sport shoes, sun protection lotion, hat, bathing suit and towel, cameras, binoculars, goggles, snorkel and etc.





Question 3 : Do we need to sign a waiver ?

Yes! Like any outdoor recreational activity, there is risk involved and all participants must sign the waiver from liability form. A parent or legal guardian must sign for any minor. Waivers will be attached to your confirmation email.





Question 4 : Is there a contact number at the destination for me to call ?

Yes. Once you have a confirmed booking, all the details of your travel service provider are located on your ticket.





Question 5 : What documention files do I need to bring along for the tour ?

You must print your ticket. It is suggested that you print the brochure page from the web site and take it on your trip so that you have all the information at your fingertips upon arriving at the destination. You will need a photo ID and each passenger ages 4 years old and above.





Question 6 : How can I get directions to the departure point ?

If you need directions to your departure point, we recommend that you use an online provider of map information such as Waze and Google Maps. You will receive the departure address on your ticket. If you still need help, please call the telephone number provided on the ticket for the tour operator. The operations staff there will be best able to assist you.
