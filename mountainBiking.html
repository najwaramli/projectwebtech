Kuala Lumpur

Location: Bukit Dinding, Kuala Lumpur.
Bukit Dinding - DH Line is a 2 km moderately popular black diamond singletrack scenic trail located near
Kuala Lumpur. This mountain bike only trail can be used downhill primary and has a hard overall physical
rating with a 17 m black diamond climb. On average it takes 46 minutes to complete this trail.
Trail Type: Singletrack.
Physical Rating: Hard.
Bike Type: DH, AM.

Bukit Padang

Location: Kota Kinabalu, Sabah.
Bukit Padang is a 1 km popular blue singletrack trail located near Kota Kinabalu. This mountain bike primary
trail can be used both directions and has a moderate overall physical rating with a 2 m blue climb. On average
it takes 33 minutes to complete this trail.
Trail Type: Singletrack.
Physical Rating: Moderate.
Bike Type: DH, AM.

Bukit Padang

Location: Kedah, Malaysia.
This Is used as a warm trail to get your eye in for the other trails on the mountain.
This trail is long, steep and gnarly. Loads of roots on the ground so be aware. Very steep in some sections.
The trail is unmarked so you will need a guide or reach out to someone local to find and ride here.
Trail Type: Singletrack.
Trail usage: Biking Primary.
Direction: Downhill only.
Bike Type: DH, AM.
